What is RecipeBook?

RecipeBook is an application that allows you to store your favorite cooking recipes in order and keep them organized.

How do I create an account?

Click this link to create an account and start adding your recipes.

Can I view others people recipes?

Unfortunately not now, but it will be implemented in future updates.

Can I login or register with Facebook?

Yes, you can register and login with Facebook.

How do I add a recipe?

In the bottom left corner of your screen there is an add recipe button and as soon you click the prompted option for add a recipe will show, you just need to follow the easy steps to add a recipe.

How do I delete a recipe?

Inside your recipe page you will find the proper recipe button for delete the current recipe you are viewing.

How do I edit a recipe?

Inside your recipe page you will find the proper recipe button for edit the current recipe you are viewing and add extra ingredients and save them.

Can I share my recipes?

Yes, you can share them in your Facebook profile :D
